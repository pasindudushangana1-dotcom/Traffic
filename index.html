Operational Signals

Standardized Command Protocols for Traffic Management.

View Full Manual ->

STOP ALL TRAFFIC

ACTION: HALT_TRAFFIC_FLOW

Raise hand vertically, palm facing forward to halt incoming flow immediately.

PROCEED

ACTION: INITIATE_MOVEMENT

Clear horizontal gesture indicating traffic may safely move forward.

YIELD / CAUTION

ACTION: PREPARE_STOP

Slow down signal. Be prepared to stop for hazards or changing conditions.

PEDESTRIAN PRIORITY

ACTION: SECURE_CROSSWALK

Ensure safe passage for civilians and students at designated crosswalks.
